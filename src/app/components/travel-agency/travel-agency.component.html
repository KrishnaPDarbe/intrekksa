Who we are

Intrekk Travel Agency is a full-service travel agency, specializing in providing customized and comprehensive travel solutions to its clients. With years of experience in the travel industry, the team at Intrekk Travel Agency is committed to making every trip a memorable and enjoyable experience. The agency offers a wide range of travel services, including airfare booking, hotel reservation, car rental, and travel insurance. Our core services

Destination Planning and Itinerary Creation

The agency's travel experts can help clients plan their trips, providing suggestions for destinations, itineraries, and activities. The team will work with clients to create a personalized travel plan that meets their specific needs and interests.

Local Experiences

Intrekk Travel Agency is dedicated to helping its clients have unique and authentic experiences when they travel. To this end, the agency offers a range of local experiences, including cultural tours, food tours, and adventure activities, that allow clients to immerse themselves in the local culture and see a different side of the destination.

Tour Packages

Intrekk Travel Agency offers a variety of tour packages to popular destinations around the world. These packages include airfare, hotel accommodation, transportation, and guided tours, making it easy for clients to plan their trips and enjoy their travels

Private Transportation

The agency provides private transportation services to its clients, including airport transfers, private car rentals, and chauffeured services. This allows clients to travel in comfort and style, without having to worry about navigating unfamiliar roads or finding public transportation.

24/7 Support

The agency's travel experts are available 24/7 to answer questions, provide assistance, and resolve any issues that may arise during a client's trip. This gives clients peace of mind knowing that they can rely on the agency for support at any time, day or night.
