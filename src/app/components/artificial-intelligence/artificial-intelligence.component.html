Who we are

InTrekk Sarl is a leading provider of mobile money (Momo) cash collection services in the Cameroon market. We specialize in providing businesses and organizations with a fast, convenient, and secure way to collect payments from their customers via mobile money. Our services include integrating mobile money payment systems into businesses' existing point-of-sale terminals, online platforms, and mobile applications. This allows customers to make payments easily and securely using their mobile money accounts. We also provide businesses with a web-based dashboard, which allows them to view transaction history, track payments and manage their mobile money account.
